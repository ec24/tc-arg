What is the Training Camp?

The Training Camp is an intensive training course of 2 weeks for programming contests with theoretical classes during the morning and practice sessions during the afternoon.

The Training Camp has been hosted in six different universities since 2010. It's always during the winter break of Argentinian universities. The 2020 edition will be the eleventh edition and hosted in Universidad Nacional de La Rioja.
